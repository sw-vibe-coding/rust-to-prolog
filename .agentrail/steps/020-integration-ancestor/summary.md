VM_INIT in src/vm/vm_main.plsw zeroes MEM(0..7703) before VM_RUN, so patching MEM via cor24-run --patch writes would be overwritten. Integrating Rust-compiled bytecode onto the real VM needs upstream support (e.g., a UART-based cell loader invoked between VM_INIT and VM_RUN, or a build-time hook that substitutes a user-provided LOAD_* proc). Filing as upstream feature; this step resumes once available, or is replaced by a weaker parity-style validation.
