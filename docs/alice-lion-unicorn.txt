A girl, Alice, is walking through a mysterious forest and meets two strange creatures: a lion and a unicorn.

Each of them has a fixed pattern of telling the truth or lying:

Lion:

Lies on Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday
Tells the truth on Thursday, Friday, Saturday, Sunday
Unicorn:

Lies on Thursday, Friday, Saturday
Tells the truth on Sunday, Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday
They make the following statements:

Lion: “Yesterday I was lying.”
Unicorn: “Yesterday I was lying."
Determine the day of the week on which Alice met them.
